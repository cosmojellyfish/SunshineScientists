Sunshine Scientists

We love science. We love being outside. As a result, we started doing as much science as we can outside.

The romanticized version of 'how to do science" is to lock yourself in a windowless dungeon and spew out your best work. We've learned that bringing your computer outside produces our best work.

Here we write about science that we get excited about in a way that makes sense to us. We write about our journey of experimenting with ways to 'get it dun'. Learn more about us
